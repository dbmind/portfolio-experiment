Paul wanted an identity that stood out unapologetically — bold, legible, and built to scale. Drawing inspiration from his love of 80s aesthetics, synthwave tones, and fearless colour clashes, we created a visual language that balanced extravagance with clarity. The result: a brand that turns heads without losing its voice.

**Role:** Brand identity · Brand consultancy · Identity design
