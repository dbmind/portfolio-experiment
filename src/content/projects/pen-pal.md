This was a simple product with a clear purpose — but we knew simplicity didn't mean bland. The goal was to build a bold, memorable brand that stood out in a crowded space of everyday objects. We paired a minimal yet classic visual system with striking, high-contrast photography to elevate the product beyond utility. The result: a brand that felt intentional, design-led, and ready to live on any desk with style.

**Role:** Brand revamp · Photography direction · Brand consultancy · Identity design
