ARTRPRNR Magazine was created in collaboration with the Miami-based team led by Mike Tewel and Realty Buyers — a bold, conceptual publication bridging the worlds of real estate, fashion, and design with a strong visual edge.

The editorial design language pushed typographic contrast to the foreground — oversized headlines, considered white space, and a visual rhythm that felt as considered as a gallery layout. Each spread was designed to feel like an art object as much as a piece of journalism.
