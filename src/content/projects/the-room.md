As Senior Designer at The Influence Room (previously, as shown here, The Room), I led a full brand revamp, helping shape the visual identity of a platform connecting creators and brands through real value exchange. I worked directly with the CEO and founders, acting as the go-to for all things design — from strategic brand direction to day-to-day execution. In a fast-paced SaaS environment, design wasn't just support — it was a driver of clarity, consistency, and growth.

**Role:** Brand Identity · UI Design · Presentation design · Social media — London, UK (2020–2021)
